Second Door

From compound interest to first principle thinking, every so-called big problem ends up being a set of small problems stacked together.

Decisions stake up the same way.

Every decision you make opens a door for a new set of decisions that also means every decision you make today was dependent upon what decision you made yesterday.

Knowing which second door your decision unlocks is crucial

You might make the best decision in a situation now but you also have to make a decision to be in a situation to make the best decision.

It's not a puzzle, all you need to do is to understand what decisions in past has led you to be in this situation to make a decision now and can you change the past decision and get into a new situation, this unlocks new decisions for you.
