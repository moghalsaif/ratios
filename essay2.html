Storyteller

Every cult, political leader, religion uses stories to influence people with stories.

The reason why this works is because people imagine themselves in the other person's story and feel the emotions and follow them irrationally.

Every leader sold an emotion that the people in the country desperately needed and Every decision they make is linked to the story.

Stories are also a way to pass on wisdom, it's either your story or someone else telling you their story to learn from.

Stories influence us like nothing else and when a story makes us a part of it, we are now sold to it.

Next time, when you want people to believe in your decisions, tell them a story behind it.
